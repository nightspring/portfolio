About me section






My web development journey began in 2015 when my interest in cryptocurrencies led me to learn more about computer science and programming. I signed up with Codecademy and quickly became hooked on coding. I started by refreshing the HTML and CSS skills I learned in high school, and then moved on to new areas like JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap and GitHub. I further solidified these front end skills by completing FreeCodeCamp's Front End Development Certification in early 2017.

Most recently I have been branching out into the back end world, and recently finished a project built with Node.js, Express.js, and a MySQL database. My goal is to become an experienced full stack developer and contribute to projects that will improve the world.

My areas of strength include design and user experience, writing clean and concise code, a good attitude, and a willingness to learn new things. Feel free to email me at codyrayhunt@gmail.com. I'd love to hear from you about working on your next project.
